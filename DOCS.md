# Ammal Farm Project Documentation

## 1. Business & Product Documents
### Business Requirements Document (BRD)
- **Objective**: Establish an online presence for Ammal Farm to attract livestock buyers and poultry enthusiasts.
- **Goals**: Lead generation via WhatsApp/Call, brand building, and farm visit scheduling.
- **Strategy**: Luxury/Premium feel for livestock (Goats) without showing prices to encourage direct interaction.

### Product Requirements Document (PRD)
- **Features**:
  - Hero section with farm vision.
  - Livestock Catalog (Goats, Chicken, Turkey, Ducks).
  - Product Catalog (Eggs).
  - Seasonal Availability indicators.
  - Booking system for farm visits.
  - Social proof (Instagram integration/link).
  - Contact system (WhatsApp, Phone, Google Maps).

---

## 2. Domain-Specific (Goat Farm) Documents
### Livestock Catalog Specification
- **Goats**: 
  - **Nellore Judipi**: Known as Bakrid goats, premium quality.
  - **Salem Black**: Local hardy livestock.
- **Poultry**: Country Chicken, Black Chicken (Kadaknath style), Turkey, Ducks.
- **Eggs**: Fresh farm eggs (Chicken, Turkey, Duck).

### Availability & Seasonality
- **Turkeys/Ducks**: Limited availability based on laying periods.
- **Goats**: Year-round availability with seasonal peaks for Bakrid.

---

## 3. Requirement Engineering (SRS)
- **Functional**:
  - Image gallery for all livestock.
  - Responsive booking form.
  - Instant WhatsApp redirection.
- **Non-Functional**:
  - Performance: Optimized images for fast loading.
  - UI/UX: Mobile-first earthy design.
  - Accessibility: High contrast for readability.

---

## 4. UI/UX Design System
- **Colors**:
  - Primary: `#2D5A27` (Forest Green)
  - Secondary: `#FDFBF7` (Almond Cream)
  - Accent: `#8B4513` (Saddle Brown)
- **Typography**: 
  - Headers: Playfair Display / Serif for "heritage" feel.
  - Body: Inter / Sans-serif for clarity.

---

## 5. Deployment & Technical
- **Tech Stack**: React 19, Tailwind CSS 4, Framer Motion.
- **Security**: Basic form validation, no sensitive data stored client-side.
- **SEO**: Meta tags for "Goat farm near me", "Nellore goat sale India".
